About Me

Interests & Skills

Mohseen is an aspiring Software Engineer with interests in Energy & Sustainability, Agile, and Technology Consulting. Through his degree in Physics, and a host of extracurricular pursuits which include studying the Arabic language, Mohseen has developed strong problem-solving and critical analysis skills. He has led and overlooked multiple teams and projects as the president of a UCL society in 2018/19 and is presently a board member of a local community organisation. Mohseen’s desire to create impactful change led him to pivot to the world of technology and join Accenture. He has since joined the UKI Sustainability Network team, tasked with demystifying Accenture’s sustainability landscape for analysts. Mohseen is keen for opportunities that will grow his skills across the technology landscape, while also furthering his functional skillset. In this pursuit, he has enrolled on the Trilogy Skills Bootcamp in Front-End Web Development. The curriculum covers HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, React.js, ES6, and Node. The course also delves into application programming interfaces (APIs), user experience (UX) design, and building and deploying modern web apps.
